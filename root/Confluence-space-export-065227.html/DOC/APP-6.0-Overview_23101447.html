Overview

This is basically a complete revamp of our existing Android application. This decision has been an outcome of a two strategies:

a) We wish to position ShoeKonnect as a marketplace platform product and not as an agent. Eg: Zomato, Quickr etc

b) We want to expand to the potential users who are unable to use our existing product offering efficiently. Therefore, increasing flexibility in ways of buying and selling.

The new release will be a complete overhaul of the existing product. We will plan to phase out some features in subsequent releases. 

Goals

  • Give users the experience that buyers are actually dealing with sellers and ShoeKonnect is simply providing the platform and services.
  • Improve Page Loading Performance
  • Allow buying based on Cartons and MOQ concepts
  • Introducing Seller MOQ feature for Wholesale Sellers
  • Implementing Google Analytics in a better way to organize meaningful data.
  • Allowing Users to explore more products by reducing Dead-end pages
  • URL Based Deep Linking (for Using Web-Engage at full potential and Marketing purposes)
