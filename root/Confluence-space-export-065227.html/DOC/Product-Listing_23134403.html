Other Features

  1. Load minimum of 20 variants in one call.
  2. The new Filter bar will appear at the bottom (This can be postponed to subsequent release)
  3. On click of any product, check the availability of the product. If not available, show toast "Product not available" (This issue can come due to periodic caching)


Logic to check availability

  1. isAvailable (variants)
  2. isSellerActive (user_master)
  3. isProductActive (user_master)
  4. isBrandActive (don't know the table name here)
  5. Limited Stock ('products' in case of Carton, 'variants_size_stock' in case of Lot)
