Documentation : Track Consignment

Created by Siddharth Rastogi, last modified on Jun 05, 2018

Overview

This page will open on clicking "Track Consignments" menu option from the side Drawer. This screen basically will help the buyers keep a track on the status of their consignments of all the orders that they have placed. This page will be divided in three tabs:

  1. In Transit: Show all consignments where: (cm.pickupInitiatedOn>0 AND cm.actDispatchDate>0 AND cm.rtoDate=0 AND cm.actDeliveryDate=0)
  2. Delivered: Show all consignments where: (cm.pickupInitiatedOn>0 AND cm.actDispatchDate>0 AND cm.rtoDate=0 AND cm.actDeliveryDate>0)
  3. RTO: Show all consignments where: (cm.pickupInitiatedOn>0 AND cm.actDispatchDate>0 AND cm.rtoDate>0)

Each tab will show individual consignment cards with following data points: 

Data Points and Logic

#Data Points and ButtonsLogic
1Seller NameThis populates the name of the seller of the consignment
2Consignment IDThis is the SK Consignment ID which appears just below the seller name.
3Date 

This is a dynamic date. Its label will change according to the tab in which the consignment is:

  1. In Transit Tab: Show text "Dispatched" followed by the date cm.actDispatchDate
  2. Delivered Tab: Show text "Delivered" followed by the date cm.actDeliveryDate
  3. RTO Tab: Show text "RTO On" followed by the date cm.rtoDate
4Status & Remarks

The status comprises of two parts: Main Status & Logistics Remarks. Each Main Status will be appended by the Logistics remarks which will be fetched from "cm.logisticsTrackingRemark" field.

  1. In Transit
    1. Logic: (cm.actDispatchDate>0 AND cm.NDRDate=0 AND cm.logisticTrackingRemark != "Out For Delivery")
    2. Color: #FF9C4B
  2. OFD
    1. Logic: (cm.actDispatchDate>0 AND cm.logisticTrackingRemark = "Out For Delivery")
    2. Color: #4BACFF
  3. NDR
    1. Logic: (cm.actDispatchDate>0 AND cm.NDRDate>0 AND cm.logisticTrackingRemark != "Out For Delivery")
    2. Color: #FF6565
  4. Delivered
    1. Logic: (cm.actDispatchDate>0 AND cm.actDeliveryDate>0 AND cm.rtoDate=0)
    2. Color: #00C29C
  5. RTO
    1. Logic: (cm.actDispatchDate>0 AND cm.rtoDate>0)
    2. Color: #FF6565

Also, there will be an info icon on the right side of the remarks status. On click, open a tooltip which will display the full remarks text. 

** Note for Iqbal - There will be a new status "OFD" which is supposed to be shown while the consignment is in "In Transit" Tab. To show this status, we need to check our logistics partner's documentation for what remark they give in case of OFD. If the cm.logisticsTrackingRemark has that value then we show the "OFD" Status on App.
