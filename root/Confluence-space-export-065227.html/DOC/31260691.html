Documentation : Dispatched (old)

Created by Siddharth Rastogi, last modified on Dec 10, 2018

Overview

This page will be for the sellers to track the statuses of all the consignments that have been Dispatched from their end. This page will be divided into 3 parts:

  1. In Transit 
  2. Delivered
  3. RTO

The structure if this page will also be the same as "Initiated Pickup" Page. Collapsed Consignment Rows and on expanding the collapsed products rows will open up. 

Data Points & Logic

S.NoData Points / ButtonLogic
1SummaryTotal Consignments: This will show the count of all the consignments that have ever been dispatched by the seller. 
2Tabs 

Page will have three tabs:

  1. In Transit - Count of consignments whose "actDispatchDate > 0" AND "actDeliveryDate = 0" AND "RtoDate = 0"
  2. Delivered - Count of consignments whose "actDispatchDate > 0" AND "actDeliveryDate > 0" AND "RtoDate = 0"
  3. RTO - Count of consignments whose "RtoDate > 0"
3Consignment Row

Showing Data Points:

  1. Buyer Name and City [Existing]
  2. Consignment ID [Existing]
  3. Consignment Value: Total Consignment Value (SK Price x Pairs), Total Products in consignment, Total pairs in consignment) [Existing]
  4. Date: This Date will change based on different tabs (Explained Below)
  5. Delhivery Partner - Name of logistics partner assigned for that consignment. [Existing]
  6. Status: The Status text will be different based on different tabs (Explained Below)
  7. Documents Tab: On click of the documents tab, following options will appear: [Existing]
    - Invoice (Download)
    - Consignment Sticker (Download)
    - Print Item List (open in print preview format)
  8. Expand/Collapse Button – for expanding the view of any consignment. [New]

4Status Tooltip

This tooltip will appear by hovering on the status text info icon. The content of this tooltip will be:

  1. Waybill No. of the consignment
  2. Status: Based on Tabs
  3. Location: As specified by the Logistics tracking API

1. In Transit

Refer image on the right for In Transit tab.

This page will show all the consignments who fall under following condition:

"actDispatchDate > 0" AND "actDeliveryDate = 0" AND "RtoDate = 0"


Date: On this tab show the "actDispatchDate"

Status: On this tab there can be three statuses:

  1. Dispatched - (if cm.actDispatchDate >0 AND ndrDate =0)
  2. Out for Delivery  
  3. NDR [If "NDRdate > 0"]

2. Delivered

Refer image on the right for Delivered Tab.

This page will show all the consignments who fall under following condition:

"actDispatchDate > 0" AND "actDeliveryDate > 0" AND "RtoDate = 0"


Date: On this tab show the "actDeliveryDate"

Status: On this tab there can be only one status:

  1. Delivered

3. RTO

Refer image on the right for RTO Tab.

This page will show all the consignments who fall under following condition:

"RtoDate > 0"


Date: On this tab show the "RtoDate"

Status: On this tab there can be two status:

  1. RTO
  2. RTO Delivered [If "actDeliveryDate > 0"]
