Documentation : Dashboard Analytics

Created by Siddharth Rastogi, last modified on Apr 10, 2018

Overview

This is the main page that will appear after logging in to the Admin Panel 2.0 This page aims to display an overall business analytics and general health of operations for all teams.

The top 3 cards:
a. Orders Placed - Total No. and Value of orders placed till date
b. Orders Confirmed - Total No. & Value of orders confirmed till date
c. Orders Cancelled - Total No. & Value of orders cancelled till date
Respectively, the graph will be a bar graph, where each bar will represent three values : Confirmed, Pending & Cancelled .
*Note - Keep the Order Place Date as an anchor and check for confirmations & cancellations for those orders only. i.e. each bar graph will show total orders placed on a particular day and confirmation and cancellation will be a subset of those orders.
Average Buyer/Seller Size
a. Avg. Buyer Size - Value of Orders Confirmed/No.Buyers
b. Avg Seller Size - Value of Orders Confirmed orders/No. of sellers)
Respectively, the graph will be a Line Graph, where each line will represent the avg. size cumulative for a particular day and all the previous days.
New vs repeat buyers will show the daily data of numbers of orders placed by each. There is a month level data filter at the top.
[Note - we may need to save this Data at the end of every day for unbiased data.
Last four graphs show the following data.
a. The cards on the top of all four graphs will show the total Count till date while the graphs will show the data according to the date range selected by the user.
b. The SKUs sold is the number of SKUs sold i.e on Orders placed on a particular day and out of those , orders that got confirmed
c. SKUs cancelled is the number of SKUs cancelled by buyers, sellers or admin
d. Sellers added data is the number of sellers added, therefore the data on the top card would be total number of sellers till date (Might have to save Seller Approved On Data)
e. Products added data is the number of products made live till date. (i.e approvedOn Date)
