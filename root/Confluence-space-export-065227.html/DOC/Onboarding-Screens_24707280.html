Documentation : Onboarding Screens

Created by Siddharth Rastogi on Mar 24, 2018

Overview

The attempt is to pass off the most important and most relevant information to the user and register a great first impression.

The goal is to:

  1. Educate users about ShoeKonnect App and problem that it is solving.
  2. Filtering out Non-targetted users and B2C users by showing the B2B aspect of the app.

Structure

The screen will be divided in two parts:

  1. Upper Part : Will have the content and Sliding images. The view pager should slide automatically as well as manually.
